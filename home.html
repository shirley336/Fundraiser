Connect

Need money to fund upcoming activities? Takes forever to contact the local businesses for fundraisers? Smaller turnout than expected? Well, let it be any of the above, WE HAVE THE SOLUTION!

Founded in 2017, Connect is an online fundraising scheduling site that connects on campus organizations to local businesses. Our mission is to provide a platform that introduces convenient, hassle-free, enjoyable, and most importantly, HIGHLY PROFITABLE fundraisers for all organizations here at UCSD. With Connect, scheduling fundraisers can be done right at your fingertips--just select the local business you want to partner up with, and we will do the rest--it's magic!
